Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

AzerbaijanFest is a cultural celebration that showcases the rich traditions, festivals, and arts of Azerbaijan, connecting locals and visitors through immersive experiences.

You can participate by checking our events calendar on the website, registering for workshops, or purchasing tickets for concerts and festivals.

Workshops are designed for all ages. However, some workshops may have specific age recommendations. Please check the individual workshop descriptions for details.

Yes! We welcome volunteers to help with various aspects of the events. Please contact us through the 'Contact Us' section for more information.

To become a sponsor, please reach out to us via the 'Contact Us' section, and we will provide more details on how you can support and be part of AzerbaijanFest.

Some events may have an entrance fee, while others may be free. Please check the event details on our events page for information regarding tickets and fees.

Pets are generally not allowed at most events for safety and comfort reasons. However, certain outdoor festivals may allow pets. Please refer to individual event details for more information.

We encourage individuals to get involved by attending events, volunteering, and joining our workshops. Community members can also help by spreading the word about AzerbaijanFest and promoting cultural engagement in their local areas.
